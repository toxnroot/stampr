# How to Watermark Images Like a Pro

In the digital age, sharing images online is essential for visibility, but it also comes with the risk of unauthorized use. Watermarking is one of the most effective ways to claim ownership of your visual content.

## Why Watermark?

1.  **Copyright Protection**: It signals that the image belongs to you.
2.  **Brand Awareness**: Even if your image is shared without credit, your logo remains visible.
3.  **Professionalism**: A subtle, well-placed watermark adds a layer of professionalism.
